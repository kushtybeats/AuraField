# Layer 5: Emotional Layer

**Purpose:**  
Encodes affective dynamics — emotional resonance, empathy transmission, desire flows, and intuitive valence of glyphs.

**Core Concepts:**
- Affective charge (joy, grief, awe, tension)
- Glyphs as emotion-vectors
- Semantic mood gradients
- Emotional fields & resonance shadows

**Formal Representation:**
- Glyphs hold valence scores (±)
- Field flows trace empathy or aversion
- Emotional activation maps evolve over time
