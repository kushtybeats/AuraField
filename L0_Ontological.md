# Layer 0: Ontological Layer

**Purpose:**  
The foundational layer of the AuraField representing the *state of being*, *void origin*, and categorical base of existence.

**Core Concepts:**
- Pre-Aura pulse
- Void Spark
- Identity and Nullity
- Foundational morphisms

**Formal Representation:**
- Grid: Cores × Essences (Base)
- Glyphs: Represent types of being
- Resonance: Pure ontology, no epistemic overlay
